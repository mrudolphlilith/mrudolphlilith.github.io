On the algebra of gauge invariants for one-flavour chromodynamics

J. Kijowski, Gerd Rudolph, Michael Rudolph

Rep. Math. Phys. 40: 131-142, 1997

Abstract

The structure of the algebra of gauge invariant differential forms built from SU(3)-gauge potentials as well as (Grassmann-algebra-valued) quark and antiquark fields is discussed. The relevance for one-flavour chromodynamics is outlined.
